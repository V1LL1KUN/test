Our Team

The $PEACE Token project was founded by two friends, one from Russia and the other from Ukraine. They met during a cultural exchange program and quickly became close friends, sharing a common vision for a peaceful world. Despite political tensions and separation, they continued to communicate daily, dreaming of a future where people from all nations could live in harmony. Their friendship inspired them to create the $PEACE Token, a symbol of hope and unity in adversity. With your support, they aim to turn this dream into reality, bringing peace and prosperity to communities globally.
